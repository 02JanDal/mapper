Edit menu

Editing is the process of marking the map with the features you wish to show, and adjusting the position and character of those features to provide a clear representation of the significant features on the ground.

Undo

ctrl + z

This most important function reverses the last change made to the map. Repeating 'Undo' will undo earlier changes, one step at a time. This will undo items created and will also restore items erased (particularly valuable for disaster moments).

Redo

ctrl + y

When you have taken Undo too far this will reverse the process — again one step at a time.

Editing tools

OOMapper offers many editing tools which may be accessed through the Tools menu or the toolbar.
